## Prime Top 10 Audio Capture Software for Spotify Users

Spotify is the most popular online music streaming platform. You will need an active internet connection to listen to your favorite music. However, the premium users can download any audio file so that they can listen to them offline. But such downloaded files can only be played on Spotify and not in any other media file streaming application. As for free users, there is no option to download audio files. That is where a **Spotify recorder** can be useful for free users.

When you record Spotify music using a third-party Spotify audio recorder, you can play such recorded files in any and all audio players. That is why a Spotify audio recorder is equally useful for premium users. After purchasing Spotify premium membership, if you do not want to pay for a high-quality Spotify audio recorder, you can install **Spotify recorder free**. Here is the list of the best free and premium Spotify recorders for you to choose from.

### 1\. Free Sound Recorder(<https://www.freesoundrecorder.net/>)

Free Sound Recorder has been the best free sound recording tool for both Windows and Mac users. You can capture not only Spotify music but also any sound that you are playing on your computer. This means you can record streaming audio, conferences, VoIP calls, audio and video calls on apps like Skype, and much more. Along with Spotify music, you can record YouTube music. ![free sound recorder](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/free-sound-recorder.jpg)

**Key Features -**

Record Everything - You can literally record anything and everything that passes through your sound card. Starting from online audio broadcasts and calls to offline sound and audio from movies, you can capture them all.

Schedule Recording - You can set a schedule whereby you can select a source for recording and set a time to start and stop recording. The software will automatically start recording as per the defined time duration, and you can do so on a daily basis. This feature is applicable to sound sources from desktop applications and websites. It is highly useful to record podcasts and programs that come at predefined scheduled times.

Audio Editor - Free Sound Recorder lets you edit the recorded audio easily. Apart from trim and crop functions, you can add special effects such as Echo, Amplify, Fade in and Fadeout, chorus, and much more.

Preset Parameters - The application automatically adjusts the parameters as per the audio format you choose. Besides, you can customize these parameters as per your requirements for perfect recording and audio output.

Easy To Use - There are only three steps to take record anything on the screen. First, you have to select the source for recording. Second, you select the preset parameters, and lastly, click on Record button to start recording. The recorded file will be saved automatically.

**How to Record from Spotify using Free Sound Recorder?**

Step 1: Install and launch Free Sound Recorder.

Step 2: Go to Recording Mixer and Recording Device and select the sound source and source card from the drop-down list.

Step 3: Adjust the audio parameters for recording such as volume and balance.

Step 4: Click on Options and select the audio file format you want.

Step 5: Finally, click on Record icon and start the audio on Spotify that you want to record. When you need to stop, click on Stop icon and the recorded audio will be saved automatically and you can see where the file is saved.

### 2\. Audacity(<https://www.audacityteam.org/>)

Audacity is an open-source audio software, and it is compatible with Windows, macOS, Linux, and other platforms. It is completely free to use, and you can record digital audio such as Spotify and YouTube as well as audio coming from microphone and mixer. It supports up to 32-bit sound quality.

The recorded audio can be exported into different file formats for playing on different players. The software allows different plug-ins for better recording and adding different effects. Besides, you can edit the recording as per requirements.

![audacity audio recorder](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/audacity-recorder.jpg)

**Key Features -**

* Record digital audio as well as live audio.
* Support plug-ins for better recording and effects.
* Spectrogram mode for visualization for professionals.
* Large set of keyboard shortcuts to control recording.
* Export recorded files in different formats.

**How to Use Audacity?**

Step 1: Install and launch Audacity.

Step 2: Go to Recording Device tab and select the source by clicking on the drop-down menu option. The menu list contains all the available recording sources on your computer.

Step 3: Make other adjustments as required and finally, click on Record button located at the top-left corner.

Step 4: When you want to stop recording, click on Stop button located beside the Record or Pause button.
