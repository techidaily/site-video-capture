Let us look at various steps of using the EZ grabber software: -

##### Step 1 Recording

The application of **EZ grabber mac**'s primary purpose would be using it for recording the content displayed on your screen. It saves the content in the form of MPEG to save space. Press the record button to start the recording and press the same to stop it. It would save the file by default.
